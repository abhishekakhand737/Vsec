Our Story

A Legacy of Authentic Flavors

🍛
18+ Years of Excellence

Khana Jo Dil Ko Chhu Jaaye

Abhishek Restaurant was born from a simple dream — to bring the soulful, time-honored recipes of Indian kitchens to every plate. Our chefs use age-old techniques, hand-ground spices, and the freshest locally sourced ingredients.

From the smoky aroma of our clay tandoor to the slow-simmered richness of our gravies, every bite carries the warmth of a home-cooked Indian meal.

  • Fresh Daily Ingredients
  • Traditional Clay Tandoor
  • Hand-Ground Spices
  • Pure Desi Ghee Used
  • Vegetarian Friendly
  • Hygienic Kitchen
